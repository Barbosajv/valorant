Valorant

Valorant is a competitive first-person shooter game developed by Riot Games. In Valorant, two teams of five players each, the attacking and defending team, compete against each other to complete objectives. The objective of the attacking team is to plant a spike at one of the designated sites on the map and defend it until it detonates. The defending team, on the other hand, must prevent the attacking team from planting the spike or defuse it if it has already been planted.
